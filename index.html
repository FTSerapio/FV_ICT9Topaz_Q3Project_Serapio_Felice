Featured Artist:

Lana Del Rey


About Artist

Lana Del Ray is a singer who now known for her alt-pop songs that usually depict a life of glamour and melancholy. She was born as Elizabeth Woolridge Grant and was raised in Lake Placid with wealthy parents. She learned how to play guitar from her uncle after she graduated highschool and began singing in clubs under the name "Lizzy Grannt".

In 2006 she signed with 5 Points Records and recorded an EP called "Kill Kill" which aired vigitally in 2008.

She started creating her Lana Del Ray Persona when she moved to England and started making youtube videos of her songs "Video Games" and "Blue Jeans". By fall, she was able to release "Video Games" under Stranger Records and won "The Next Big Thing Throphy."

She then released her first album "Born to Die" to mixed reviews. Later that year, she released the "Paradie" EP and bundled it with "Born to Die".

Throughout 2013, she released many different singles and videos including a cover of Leonard Cohen's "Chelsea Hotel #2", Lee Hazlewood "Summer Wine" with her boyfriend Barrie-James O'Neill, but her biggest release has to be "Young and Beautiful". All of these, including her cover of "Once Upon a Dream" for Disney's malificent kept her in the spotlight until she released her 2nd album.

She hired Dan Auerbach to produce the majority of Ultraviolence, her 2nd album. This album had both better reviews and sales compared to "Born to Die". It didn't have as many hits as "Born to Die", however this helped elevate Lana Del Ray's reputation.

In 2015, she worked on another album called Honeymoon with the singles "High by the Beach" and "Terrence Loves You". This was able to reach number 2 in Billboards 200. In early 2017, she released "Love", a single for her next full length album called "Lust for Life". This debuted at number 1 in Billboards top 200 and gave her her second grammy nomination for Best Pop Vocal Albim.

She then released the singles "Mariners Apartment Complex" and "Venice Bitch" for her fifth album "Normal Fucking Rockwell!". This album received Grammy nominations for Album of the Year and Song for the Year for it's title track. In 2021, she released Chemtrails over the Country Club. In 2022 She she landed on Top 40 of Hot Rock & Alternative Songs chart with "Did you know that there's a tunel under Ocean Blvd".
